About Us

Cakes n Bakes 365 is a family-run bakery and fast food destination in Mehrauli. We started with a single oven and a love for sharing sweet moments with neighbors. Today we blend traditional baking with quick-serve favorites to keep every visit warm, comforting, and memorable.

Every cake, pastry, and plate is prepared with care, using ingredients we trust and recipes refined over the years. When you order from us, you are supporting local talent and a small team that believes in honest flavors and friendly service.
